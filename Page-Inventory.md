## Block Reusability Analysis

**Highest Reuse Blocks:**
- Hero blocks: Used on 200+ pages (service, location, about pages)
- CTA/Quote banners: Used on 250+ pages
- Service grids: Used on 100+ pages
- Footer: Used on all 300+ pages

**Medium Reuse Blocks:**
- FAQ: Used on 50+ service pages
- Client logos: Used on 20+ pages
- Statistics: Used on 10+ pages

**Low Reuse Blocks:**
- Blog layouts: Used on blog section only (~150+ posts)
- Team profiles: Used on 1 page
- Awards: Used on 1-2 pages

---

## Recommendation
Focus on **Priority 1 blocks first** as they cover 80% of the website's pages. These blocks have the highest reuse potential and will allow you to build out most service and location pages quickly once complete.
